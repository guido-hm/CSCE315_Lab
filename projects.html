Medical Store Inventory

A python app that uses the tkinter GUI module and postgreSQL database used to keep track of medical equipment at a store. This app is used with uniquely generated barcodes for each piece of equipment and allows users to enter equipment into the inventory, remove inventory by scanning the barcode, look up inventory, and see what was in store on any given date.
