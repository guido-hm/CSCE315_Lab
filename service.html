CAMP Camp

In the summer of 2019, I volunteered at a summer camp named CAMP camp. CAMP camp is a place where both children and adults with various kinds of disabilities go to enjoy a summer camp specially made with them and their disabilities in mind. I spent a week tagging along to one of the campers, going anywhere they wanted to go, doing all types of activities with them, and making sure he had a blast. Click HERE to learn more.
